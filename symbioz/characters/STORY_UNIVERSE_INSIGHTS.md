1. **Amber Channeling Events:**
   - Reality-bending, unpredictable
   - Can trap people in impossible states
   - Requires containment, not control
   - Pattern: Feared, rare, consequential

2. **Honey Mutations:**
   - Permanent alterations (Reth's case)
   - Unpredictable biological reactions
   - Risk vs. reward trade-offs
   - Pattern: Dangerous but potentially powerful

3. **Amber Scars:**
   - Permanent reminders (Vex)
   - Physical/mental changes
   - Enhanced understanding but ongoing consequences
   - Pattern: Cost of knowledge

- Honey smuggling (Kaelen's father, Thorn)
- Illegal honey consumption for enhancement
- Black market Honey trade (Cartel operations)
- Underground resistance networks

### Lost/Mysterious Elements:

- Ancient Channeler sites (Vex references)
- Lost civilizations with Amber technology
- Ancient inscriptions (Vex's expertise)
- "Biosphere secrets" (Mara references)

### Vision/Mystical Experiences:

- Not heavily referenced in current stories
- Reth's "hive consciousness" awareness (biological, not mystical)
