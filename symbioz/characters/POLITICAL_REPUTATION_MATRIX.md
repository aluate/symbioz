## Gameplay System Integration

### Reputation Mechanics:

**Pricing Modifiers:**
- Positive reputation: -10% to -25% prices
- Negative reputation: +25% to +100% prices (or refusal)
- Neutral: Standard prices

**NPC Reactions:**
- Positive: Friendly, helpful, offer information
- Negative: Hostile, suspicious, may refuse service
- Neutral: Standard interactions

**Mission Availability:**
- Positive: Access to exclusive missions
- Negative: Mission restrictions or refusal
- Neutral: Standard mission access

**Black Market Access:**
- Positive: Access granted
- Negative: Denied or high risk
- Neutral: Standard access
