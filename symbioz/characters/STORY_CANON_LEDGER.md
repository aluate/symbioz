## Honey Types

| Honey Type | Source Planet | Mechanical Buffs | Rarity |
|------------|---------------|------------------|--------|
| **Ironflower Honey** | Kessar Prime | Strength-enhancing, permanent developmental enhancement when consumed during growth years | Common (illegal in raw/unprocessed form) |
| **Crag-Nectar Honey** | Deepstone | Mining stamina enhancement, grants extended endurance for holding positions | Native/common |
| **Luminescent Pollen Honey** | Veil-Spire | Sensory enhancement (heightens awareness of vibrations, sounds, air pressure changes), grants bonuses to perception and stealth | Native/common |
| **Symbiotic Bloom Honey** | Symbiote's Rest | Neural interface enhancement, allows seamless connection with cybernetic systems, grants bonuses to tech-related skill checks | Native/common |
| **Tide-Petal Honey** | Aquila Depths | Underwater respiration enhancement, environmental adaptation, grants bonuses to survival and ecosystem analysis | Native/common (submerged hives) |
| **Metallic-Ash Honey** | Tri-Station Gamma | Machine interface enhancement, grants bonuses to ancient technology analysis | Native/common |
| **Sand-Sting Honey** | Dune-Scar | Multi-task enhancement, allows simultaneous monitoring and coordination, grants bonuses to battlefield control | Native/common |
| **Stone-Root Honey** | Gravitas Core | Mass stabilization, gravitational adaptation, grants bonuses to presence and stability in negotiations | Native/common |
| **Refractive Nectar Honey** | Crystal Void | Light manipulation enhancement, grants bonuses to stealth and optical camouflage | Native/common |
| **Collective Nectar** | Unknown (likely Yntari-related) | **CAUSES PERMANENT MUTATION** - hypersensitivity to biological systems, hive consciousness awareness, sensory overload near large populations | Rare/dangerous |

## Factions

| Faction | Ideology | Major Conflicts | Territory |
|---------|----------|-----------------|-----------|
| **Wardens of the Reach** | Neutral protector faction | Maintains order, monitors Amber-related anomalies, protects civilians, contains dangerous forces | Neutral stations, observation posts, waystations (including The Spindle) |
| **Horizon Combine** | Corporate power, expansionist | Controls frontier worlds, uses legal manipulation and force, maintains company towns and processing facilities | Frontier worlds, processing facilities, research stations, company towns |
| **Horizon Combine (Research Division)** | Scientific advancement within corporate structure | Pushes research boundaries while maintaining corporate compliance, monitors for dangerous research | Research facilities, laboratories |
| **Horizon Combine (Security Division)** | Corporate security and control | Maintains corporate interests, eliminates threats to expansion, monitors research for violations | Corporate facilities, security outposts |
| **Confluence Cartel** | Honey distribution network, information broker | Smuggling operations, intelligence gathering, economic influence through trade | Trade routes, black markets, neutral zones, The Spindle |
| **Resistance Cells** | Anti-Combine, worker protection | Targets Combine operations, disrupts supply lines, protects settlements from corporate expansion | Independent settlements, hidden bases, operates across frontier |
| **Independent Operators** | Freelance contractors | No unified ideology, work for whoever pays, maintain independence | The Spindle, freelance contracts across all territories |
| **Aeshura Culture** | Honor-bound precision combat | Values ranged combat, honor duels, precision strikes, rejects infiltration as dishonorable | Veil-Spire, independent communities |
| **Scientific Community** | Research and knowledge | Values ethical methodology, understands Honey/Amber interactions, independent research | Various research facilities, academic networks |
