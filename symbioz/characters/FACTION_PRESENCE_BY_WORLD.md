# Faction Presence by World

**Purpose:** Reference for which factions control/operate on which planets  
**Use Cases:** Mission design, territory mechanics, conflict zones, economic interests  
**Status:** Complete

## Planets and Faction Presence

### Kessar Prime
- **Primary Control:** Horizon Combine
- **Faction Presence:**
  - Combine: Full control (company towns, processing facilities, security forces)
  - Workers: Indigenous population, exploited
  - Resistance Cells: Underground operations
- **Economic Interests:** Ironflower honey processing, worker labor
- **Conflict Zones:** Worker uprisings, Combine security operations
- **Key Features:** Company town structure, worker exploitation

### Deepstone
- **Primary Control:** Independent (Stonelock colony)
- **Faction Presence:**
  - Stonelock Population: Indigenous, mining culture
  - Combine: Recruitment operations (security personnel)
  - Independents: Native population
- **Economic Interests:** Mining, Crag-Nectar honey production
- **Conflict Zones:** Minimal (internal mining operations)
- **Key Features:** Mining culture, dangerous core mining

### Veil-Spire
- **Primary Control:** Independent (Aeshura colony)
- **Faction Presence:**
  - Aeshura Population: Indigenous, honor-based culture
  - Cartel: Minimal (operatives may visit)
- **Economic Interests:** Luminescent Pollen honey production
- **Conflict Zones:** Internal honor duels only
- **Key Features:** Precision combat culture, honor-based society

### Symbiote's Rest
- **Primary Control:** Independent (Sylarin world)
- **Faction Presence:**
  - Sylarin Population: Indigenous, biological integration culture
  - Combine Research: Recruits researchers (like Zara)
- **Economic Interests:** Symbiotic Bloom honey, biological research
- **Conflict Zones:** Minimal
- **Key Features:** Biological symbiosis culture

### Aquila Depths
- **Primary Control:** Independent (Ærathi ocean world)
- **Faction Presence:**
  - Ærathi Population: Indigenous, aquatic culture
  - Wardens: Recruited Pioneer (Mara)
- **Economic Interests:** Tide-Petal honey (submerged hives)
- **Conflict Zones:** Minimal
- **Key Features:** Underwater cities, aquatic adaptation

### Tri-Station Gamma
- **Primary Control:** Independent (Vazari outpost)
- **Faction Presence:**
  - Vazari Population: Indigenous, tripodal culture

- **Economic Interests:** Metallic-Ash honey, artifact recovery services

- **Key Features:** Precision work culture, artifact network connections

### Dune-Scar

## Faction Territory Claims

### Horizon Combine Territory:

**Full Control:**
- Kessar Prime (frontier world, company towns)

**Economic Influence:**
- Multiple frontier worlds (processing facilities)
- Research stations
- Company towns across frontier

**Conflict Zones:**
- Worker uprisings
- Settlement disputes
- Resistance operations

**Territorial Expansion:**
- Uses legal manipulation for land claims
- Corporate security forces
- Economic pressure

### Wardens of the Reach Territory:

**Presence:**
- Neutral stations (The Spindle, Gamma-7)
- Observation posts
- Waystations

**Mission:**
- Monitor Amber-related anomalies
- Maintain order in neutral spaces
- Protect civilians

**No Territorial Control:**
- Maintains neutral presence
- Doesn't claim territory
- Operates across all territories

### Confluence Cartel Territory:

**Operations:**
- Trade routes
- Black markets
- Neutral zones
- The Spindle (operational base)

**No Direct Territory:**
- Operates in spaces between factions
- Network-based operations
- Economic influence through trade

### Resistance Cells Territory:

**Operations:**
- Independent settlements
- Hidden bases
- Operates across Combine territory

**No Formal Territory:**
- Underground operations
- Targets Combine interests
- Protects settlements from expansion
