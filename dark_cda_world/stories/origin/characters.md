Working character list for the Origin era. Cursor should keep this file in sync with story details.

## Taman (The Boy)
- Role: Protagonist / source of Split
- Holds both coin and pouch
- Torn between obedience and resentment
- Skilled tracker; reads the ground like words
- Sensitive to "ground-hum" vibration that indicates balance
- Uses pouch (made by Sela) to steady himself when Weight builds

## Sela
- Role: Sister, Holder of Weight
- Gift: Holding
- Calm, steady, protective
- Two years older than Taman
- Made Taman's pouch from scraps
- Dark hair, braided back
- Can sense when things are about to shift
- Unintentionally steadies Taman without words

## Mother
- Role: Single parent
- Hard but loving
- Death is a key turning point

## Arpin (The Trader)
- Role: Early manipulator, proto-Arpin
- Introduces ledger thinking
- Tall, well-dressed, smile that never reaches his eyes
- Arrived at settlement six months before Chapter 1
- Uses ledgers to bind people through debt and obligation

## Boone
- Role: Friend of Taman
- Approximately one year older than Taman
- Broad-shouldered, earnest
- Takes to Arpin's ledgers quickly
- Believes numbers can solve problems
- Sees ledgers as a "map to safety"

## Isaac
- Role: Arpin's right hand man
- Quiet, moves like a shadow
- Uses hidden coin to influence people
- Coin amplifies persuasion; makes people agree to things they wouldn't normally

## The Missionary
- Role: Outsider, prophet/warning figure
- Gaunt man in dark clothes
- Moves like a shade through the settlement
- Mutters about "crooked things," tremors, weight shifting
- Speaks of the mountain watching and waiting
- People ignore him as a madman, but he sees things others don't
- Warns of scales tipping, tremors, fire, and "the split"

## Tom
- Role: Settler
- Works extra shifts for Arpin
- Disputes flour allocation with Arpin
- Falls under Isaac's coin influence

## Ruth
- Role: Tom's wife
- Worried, relieved when disputes resolve
- Falls under Isaac's coin influence

## Hiram
- Role: Blacksmith
- Big man, broad-shouldered, steady
- Speaks openly against Arpin's rule in the square
- Beaten by Arpin (backhand and iron rod) when he resists
- Locked away after first public resistance
- First person to openly challenge Arpin

## Caleb
- Role: Cooper (barrel maker)
- Argues for water storage, forward planning
- Arpin mocks him, dismisses his concerns
- Dragged away by Silas for speaking up
- Represents those trying to think ahead, prepare for future

## Silas
- Role: Arpin's enforcer
- One of Arpin's men who does "dirty work"
- Moves with cruelty
- Drags Caleb away when he speaks up
- Part of Arpin's muscle/threat system

## Mary
- Role: Settler
- Older woman, face lined with worry
- Husband is sick
- Struggling since Arpin's arrival
- Arpin "forgives" her debt, creating obligation/gratitude

## Elders
- Role: Custodians of old stories
- Know about balance and Seven Fires

## Mira
- Role: Daughter of local trader tied to Fort Shadow
- Approximately 1-2 years older than Taman
- Sharp-eyed and kind
- Knows the way things are at Fort Shadow
- Protective of her own people
- Father has friends at east fort who understand balance
- Curious about Sela's gift
- Offers to help Taman find men who understand weight and balance
