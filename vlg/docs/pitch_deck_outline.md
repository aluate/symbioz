This outline can be used to create a PowerPoint, Keynote, or other presentation deck for investor presentations.

**Content**:

- "Building an Integrated Housing Ecosystem in the Inland Northwest"
- Date
- Confidential

---

## Slide 2: The Problem

**Content**:
- Chronic housing shortages in Inland Northwest
- Rising labor costs
- Limited subcontractor availability
- Long build timelines (6–12 months typical)
- Unpredictable cabinet lead times (3–6 weeks)
- Lack of modern modular supply
- Outdated factory-built housing options
- Nearly empty ADU market

**Visual**: Problem statements, statistics, market data

---

## Slide 3: The Solution

**Content**:
- Vertically integrated housing ecosystem
- Four companies working together:
  - Valhalla Legacy Group (holding & development)
  - Sugar Mountain Builders (GC + spec homes)
  - Stax Modular (modern prefab homes)
  - Grid Cabinet Systems (fast-turn cabinets)
- Margin stacking + timeline reduction

**Visual**: Ecosystem diagram, integration flow

---

## Slide 4: Market Opportunity

**Content**:
- Inland Northwest housing market
- ADU market (nearly empty)

- Modular workforce housing
- Spec-home development
- Builder partnerships

**Visual**: Market size, growth trends, opportunity areas

## Slide 5: The Vertical Integration Flywheel

**Content**:
1. Grid → supplies Stax + SMB + retail
2. Stax → supplies SMB + builders + developers
3. SMB → installs Stax + Grid + Valhalla work
4. Valhalla → owns land + assets + infrastructure

**Benefits**:
- Margin stacking (20–40% COGS reduction)
- Timeline reduction (50–70% faster)
- Supply chain control
- Quality consistency

**Visual**: Flywheel diagram, benefit metrics

## Notes for Presentation

### Design Guidelines
- Use brand colors (see brand briefs)
- Professional, clean design
- Minimal text, strong visuals
- Consistent branding across slides

### Presentation Tips
- Tell the story (problem → solution → opportunity)
- Emphasize vertical integration advantage
- Highlight margin stacking and timeline reduction
- Show traction and progress
- Be clear on ask and use of funds
- Address risks proactively

### Customization
- Adjust slides based on audience (investors vs. partners)
- Emphasize relevant aspects for each audience
- Update with current traction and metrics
- Customize financials based on latest models
