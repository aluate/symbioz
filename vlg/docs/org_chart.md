## Organizational Relationships

### Reporting Structure

**Valhalla → Operating Companies**
- Valhalla owns assets (land, buildings, equipment)
- Operating companies lease from Valhalla
- Operating companies report to Valhalla (strategic level)
- Operating companies operate independently (operational level)

**Operating Companies → Valhalla**
- Strategic alignment
- Financial reporting
- Asset utilization
- Growth planning

### Cross-Company Collaboration

**SMB ↔ Stax**
- SMB installs Stax modules
- Stax supplies modules to SMB
- Coordinated sales
- Shared clients

**SMB ↔ Grid**
- SMB specifies Grid cabinets
- Grid supplies cabinets to SMB
- Coordinated projects
- Shared clients

**Stax ↔ Grid**
- Stax uses Grid cabinets
- Grid supplies cabinets to Stax
- Internal transfer pricing
- Ecosystem efficiency
