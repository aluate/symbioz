# Sugar Mountain Builders — Brand Brief

## Brand Purpose

Sugar Mountain Builders (SMB) delivers high-quality construction, spec homes, remodels, and development work with a mountain-modern luxury edge. SMB is the customer-facing builder in the Valhalla ecosystem.

## Positioning

**Mountain-modern craftsmanship. Dependable execution. Homes built for mountain living.**

## Color Palette

**Primary**

- Tiffany Blue — #81D8D0  
- Black — #000000  

**Secondary**

- Charcoal — #1E1E1E  
- Warm White — #F5F5F5  
- Deep Teal — #0C3B3B  

**Usage Notes**

- Tiffany Blue is the anchor brand color.  
- Use heavy black backgrounds for luxury contrast.  
- Use Warm White for approachable copy-heavy sections.

## Typography

**Primary Serif (Brand Font)**  

- **Playfair Display**  

  - Headlines  

  - Hero sections  

  - Key statements  

**Secondary Sans (Support Font)**  

- Inter (or Montserrat as fallback)  

  - Body text  

  - Buttons  

  - UI labels  

  - Navigation

## Visual Identity

- Mountain silhouette icon (clean, geometric).  
- Tiffany Blue accents against black backgrounds.  
- Photography: lakes, mountains, clean interiors, craftsmanship shots.  
- Tone: warm but confident.

## Do / Don't

**Do**

- Lean into mountain-modern design language.  
- Show craftsmanship and clean builds.  
- Present SMB as a premium, reliable GC.

**Don't**

- Use rustic lodge aesthetics.  
- Use cartoonish or overly stylized mountains.  
- Present SMB as low-cost or volume-driven.

## Brand Role in Ecosystem

- Installs Stax modular units.  
- Builds traditional homes + spec projects.  
- Executes land improvements for Valhalla.  
- Partners with Grid for cabinets.

## What SMB Is

- GC and development company.
- High-end client experience.
- Builder of spec, modular installs, and site-built homes.
- The hands and boots on the ground for Grid + Stax products.
- Expert in mountain/lake-region construction.

## What SMB Is NOT

- A modular factory.
- A cabinet manufacturer.
- Budget, low-end, or production-builder tier.

## Service Pillars

- New construction (mountain-modern emphasis).
- Mod-home installation and finish work.
- Remodels and additions.
- Site preparation, utilities, and infrastructure work.
- Development services for Valhalla-owned land.

## Tone & Voice

- Warm  
- Professional  
- Mountain-modern  
- Reliable  
- Confident  

Words we use: craftsmanship, mountain-modern, dependable, schedule, detail.  

Words we avoid: cheap, bargain, gimmicky.
