# Valhalla Legacy Group — Brand Brief

## Brand Purpose

Valhalla Legacy Group is the quiet holding and development company behind SMB, Grid, and Stax. It owns land, buildings, equipment, and long-term real assets.

## Positioning

**A quiet holding company focused on real estate, development, and long-term value creation.**

## Color Palette

**Primary**

- Obsidian — #0A0A0A  
- Aged Gold — #C4A853  

**Secondary**

- Stone Gray — #B7B7B7  
- Ivory — #FAF7EF  

**Usage Notes**

- Gold used sparingly as an institutional accent.  
- The brand should feel like a financial firm or asset manager.

## Typography

**Primary Sans (Brand Font)**  

- Inter  

- Montserrat as secondary  

- No serif (keeps it institutional and clean)

## Taglines (Approved)

- **"Assets. Development. Legacy."**

- **"Quietly Building the Backbone of the Future."**

- **"Strategic Holdings for a Mountain-Based Ecosystem."**

## Website Hero Lines

**Hero Line:**  

> *"Strategic Holdings. Long-Term Vision."*

**Subheading:**  

> *"A private development and asset-holding firm supporting the Inland Northwest's housing ecosystem."*

## Visual Identity

- Minimalist wordmark  

- Black + gold institutional palette  

- Architectural photography (factories, land, industrial improvements)  

- No mountain lifestyle visuals

## Do / Don't

**Do**

- Keep the brand extremely quiet and minimal.  
- Focus on assets, development, planning.  
- Use strong, serious tone.

**Don't**

- Use bright colors or lifestyle imagery.  
- Market directly to homeowners.  
- Present Valhalla as a builder or manufacturer.

## Brand Role in Ecosystem

- Owns all land, factories, and long-term holds.  
- Leases industrial facilities to Grid + Stax.  
- Manages development for spec villages and modular campuses.  
- Acts as investor-facing entity.

## What Valhalla Is

- Asset owner.
- Developer of industrial property.
- Capital allocator.
- Landholder for Grid + Stax campuses.
- Owner of spec homes and prototype villages.
- Quiet partner with investors.

## What Valhalla Is NOT

- Customer-facing.
- A builder, cabinet shop, or modular factory.
- A marketing-forward brand.

## Responsibilities

- Acquire land (3–5 acre parcels ideal).
- Develop industrial sites for factories and shops.
- Lease facilities to Grid and Stax.
- Build prototype villages and spec home clusters.
- Manage long-term holds for rentals or resale.

## Tone & Voice

- Ultra professional  
- Minimalist  
- Reserved  
- Institutional  
- Strategic  

Words we use: holdings, development, assets, risk mitigation, long-term, portfolio.  

Words we avoid: hype, sales language, consumer tone.
