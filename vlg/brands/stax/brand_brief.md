# Stax — Brand Brief

## Brand Purpose

Stax builds modern modular homes engineered for speed, precision, and efficient installation.

## Positioning

**Modern modular homes engineered to stack, ship, and set with precision.**

## Color Palette

**Primary**

- Deep Slate — #2C2F33  
- Warm Concrete — #A7A9AC  

**Accent**

- Electric Amber — #FFB640  
- Black — #000000  
- White — #FFFFFF  

**Usage Notes**

- Electric Amber = signature highlight color.  
- Slate + Concrete create a modern prefab aesthetic.

## Typography

**Primary Sans (Brand Font)**  

- Inter (primary)  

- Montserrat or Poppins for headings if needed

Clean, modern, geometric. No serif.

## Taglines (Approved)

- **"Engineered to Stack."**

- **"Modern Modular, Built Fast."**

- **"Prefab, But Premium."**

## Website Hero Lines

**Hero Line:**  

> *"Modern Modular Homes. Delivered Fast."*

**Subheading:**  

> *"Flat-roof prefab units engineered for stacking, transport, and rapid installation."*

## Visual Identity

- Flat-roof silhouettes  
- Long, sleek rectangles  
- Modular seams (subtle)  
- Modern, architectural photography  
- Slate + Amber color pairing

## Do / Don't

**Do**

- Highlight modern prefab design.  
- Show flat-roof modern homes like Ideabox.  
- Emphasize engineering + speed.

**Don't**

- Use traditional pitched-roof house icons.  
- Use rustic cabin imagery.  
- Present Stax like a tiny home brand.

## Brand Role in Ecosystem

- Manufactures modular units.  
- Supplies SMB with install-ready boxes.  
- Uses Grid cabinets standardized to Stax layouts.  
- Sellable standalone manufacturing company.

## What Stax Is

- Modular housing manufacturer.
- Modern, flat-roof architecture (Ideabox-inspired).
- Single, double, and triple module homes.
- Engineered for transport, stacking, and fast site install.
- Paired naturally with Grid (interiors) and SMB (install).
- Designed for housing shortages, ADUs, cabins, and efficient small homes.

## What Stax Is NOT

- RV trailers or mobile-homes.
- Handcrafted tiny houses.
- Rustic cabin kits.
- Luxury custom architecture.

## Product Line Pillars

- Modern silhouette (flat roof, big windows, clean lines).
- Highway-legal modular sizes.
- Module types: Studio, 1BR, 2BR, Flex, Multi-box homes.
- Shared interior SKUs with Grid cabinets.
- Options packages tied to Sugar Mountain spec tiers (Quartz / Garnet / Sapphire).

## Tone & Voice

- Modern  
- Optimistic  
- Engineered  
- Clean  
- Efficient  
- Future-forward  

Words we use: stacked, modular, engineered, systems, prefab, modern.  

Words we avoid: cute, quirky, rustic, whimsical.
