# Grid Cabinet Systems — Brand Brief

## Brand Purpose

Grid Cabinet Systems manufactures fast-turn, CNC-precise melamine casework for builders, remodelers, and modular manufacturers.

## Positioning

**Engineered, fast-turn cabinet systems for modern construction.**

## Color Palette

**Primary**

- Graphite Black — #111111  
- Machine Gray — #868686  

**Secondary**

- Light Steel — #DADADA  
- Safety Yellow (accent) — #FFCB2F  
- Pure White — #FFFFFF  

**Usage Notes**

- Safety Yellow should be used sparingly as an engineering/industrial accent.  
- Grayscale is the core identity.

## Typography

**Primary Sans (Brand Font)**  

- **Inter** (default)  

- Optional: Montserrat or Poppins

Use sans-serif everywhere for an engineered, technical feel.

## Taglines (Approved)

- **"System-Built. CNC-Precise."**

- **"Cabinetry That Doesn't Miss Schedules."**

- **"Fast-Turn. Engineered. Reliable."**

## Website Hero Lines

**Hero Line:**  

> *"Precise. Fast. System-Built Cabinetry."*

**Subheading:**  

> *"CNC-driven melamine cabinet systems with 4–7 day QuickBuild™ options."*

## Visual Identity

- Clean grid patterns  
- Industrial grayscale theme  
- Sharp geometric iconography  
- Photography: CNC machines, panels, melamine stacks  

## Do / Don't

**Do**

- Keep everything minimal & engineered.  
- Use modular grid or line motifs.  
- Emphasize lead time, reliability, and repeatability.

**Don't**

- Use woodgrain-heavy or artisan-style imagery.  
- Use warm, rustic colors.  
- Position Grid as custom cabinetry.

## Brand Role in Ecosystem

- Supplies cabinets to SMB and Stax.  
- Provides SKUs for modular units.  
- Operates independently and is sellable as its own company.

## What Grid Is

- Industrial, engineered, modern.
- CNC-first manufacturing.
- Standardized SKU-based cabinet systems.
- Fast-turn cabinetry with 4–7 day QuickBuild™ program.
- Supported by Strategic Stock Reserve for immediate jobs.
- Supplier to Stax and SMB.

## What Grid Is NOT

- Custom cabinetry shop.
- Rustic, luxury, or artisan focused.
- Paint/finish heavy.
- Boutique or one-off builds.

## Product Line Pillars

- Frameless Euro melamine cabinets.
- 5-color core line.
- Standardized SKUs (base, wall, tall, closet, garage).
- Two handle SKUs.
- Soft-close hardware only.
- Systemized installation standards.

## Tone & Voice

- Precise  
- Industrial  
- Direct  
- Reliable  
- Systematic  
- No fluff  

Words we use: system, grid, CNC, engineered, predictable, repeatable, fast.  

Words we avoid: artisan, handcrafted, bespoke, rustic.
