# Forge Site — Pricing Model

## Base Pricing

### Standard Site Build

**$4,500 – $6,500**

Includes:

- Template selection and site generation
- Up to 5 standard pages (home, services, about, contact, +1)
- Standard modules (hero, services grid, gallery, forms)
- Brand token application
- Basic SEO setup
- Vercel deployment assistance
- One round of revisions

## Pricing Philosophy

Forge Site pricing reflects:

- **Speed:** Sites built in days, not months
- **Quality:** Professional, tested templates and modules
- **Simplicity:** Clear process, no surprises
- **Value:** High-margin for the business, fair price for clients

Prices are ranges to allow flexibility based on:

- Complexity of requirements
- Number of revisions needed
- Timeline urgency
- Ongoing relationship potential

## Payment Terms

- **50% deposit** to start
- **50% on completion** before deployment
- **Add-ons** billed separately or added to final invoice
- **Subscriptions** billed monthly, cancel anytime

## Notes

- All prices in USD
