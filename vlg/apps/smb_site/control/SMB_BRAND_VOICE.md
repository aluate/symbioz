# SMB Brand Voice - Quick Reference

**Premium, Understated Luxury, Mountain-Modern**

## Voice Characteristics

- ✅ Premium
- ✅ Understated luxury
- ✅ Mountain-modern aesthetic
- ✅ Designed, not decorated
- ✅ Architecturally clean
- ✅ Quiet confidence
- ✅ Wellness + nature + clarity of space
- ✅ Precision, certainty, craftsmanship
- ✅ Time-efficient, chaos-resistant
- ✅ Modular as elevated construction methodology

## Tone Rules

**DO:**
- Use refined, confident language
- Emphasize certainty and reliability
- Focus on lifestyle outcomes
- Let confidence carry the tone
- Frame modular as superior methodology

**DON'T:**
- Use casual language or slang
- Add humor or jokes
- Over-sell or be pushy
- Frame modular as budget option
- Sound like a toy or DIY tool

## Key Phrases

- "Crafted with precision"
- "Delivered with certainty"
- "Where modern architecture meets alpine living"
- "Every detail considered"
- "Sophisticated design tool"
- "Curated starting point"
