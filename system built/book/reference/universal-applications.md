\# UNIVERSAL APPLICATIONS OF THE FOUR-MOVE SYSTEM



Use these to illustrate how the same method works everywhere.



---



\# MONEY

1\. Define: What do I want my financial life to be?

2\. Parts: Income, expenses, debt, savings, automation.

3\. Acquisition: Info, clarity, spreadsheets, boundaries.

4\. Loop: Review → adjust → repeat monthly.



---



\# WORK \& CAREER

1\. Define career target.

2\. Parts: skills, resume, habits, relationships.

3\. Acquire: mentors, training, clarity from bosses.

4\. Loop: monthly skill gain + quarterly strategy review.



---



\# RELATIONSHIPS

1\. Define: What do we want this relationship to be?

2\. Parts: communication, empathy, time, trust.

3\. Acquire: tools, conversations, boundaries.

4\. Loop: weekly check-ins, adjustments.



---



\# HEALTH

1\. Define “healthy enough for my life.”

2\. Parts: sleep, food quality, movement, habits.

3\. Acquire: equipment, routines, knowledge.

4\. Loop: 3-day, 7-day, 30-day check cycles.



---



\# IDENTITY

1\. Define who you want to be.

2\. Parts: habits, environment, roles, beliefs.

3\. Acquire: new skills, new people, new standards.

4\. Loop: act → evaluate → adjust → repeat.
